Light and Energy Computing

Concept and Ideological–Scientific Framework
by Vinicios Ongaratto

Abstract

Light and Energy Computing is an original research concept developed by Vinicios Ongaratto, proposing a model of computation where energy coherence, light propagation, and quantum synchronization serve as the foundation of data processing.

  • ⚡ Energy becomes the information carrier.
  • 💡 Light becomes the computational channel.
  • 🌐 Electromagnetic coherence becomes the logical structure.
